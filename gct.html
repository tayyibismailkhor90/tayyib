Three Years Diploma in Mechanical Technology

The Mechanical Engineering Technology program provides relevant industrial experience within the academic environment through laboratory projects, experimentation, classroom lecture and demonstrations. Students have access to state-of-the-art computer facilities, materials testing, power transmission, fluids, automation and manufacturing laboratories. Students gain experience with computer software (widely used in industry) for incorporation into projects, associated with key manufacturing areas typical of projects carried out in industry. The program places emphasis on mechanical design, component analysis and product development
